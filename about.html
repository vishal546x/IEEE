About the Conference

The ICSVCE 2021: International Conference on Advances in Signal Processing, VLSI, Communications and Embedded Systems aims to bring together leading academic scientists, researchers and research scholars to exchange and share their experiences and research results.

Due to rapidly changing COVID situations globally, ICSVCE 2021 will take place online to ensure the safety of all participants.

Our Mission

To provide a premier interdisciplinary platform for researchers, practitioners, and educators to present and discuss the most recent innovations, trends, and concerns.

Our Scope

Covering practical challenges encountered and solutions adopted in the fields of Signal Processing, VLSI, Communications, and Embedded Systems.

Organizing Institution

Vardhaman College of Engineering

Established in 1999, Vardhaman College of Engineering is affiliated to JNTU, Hyderabad, and approved by AICTE, New Delhi.

The campus is located near the Bangalore Highway (NH-7), just a 30-minute drive from the Hyderabad International Airport at Shamshabad.

Publication & Indexing

All accepted and presented papers are expected to be published in Springer Conference Proceedings (Approval Pending). The papers will also be submitted for indexing in Scopus.
