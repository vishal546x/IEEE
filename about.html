About the Conference

ICAIVSC 2026: International Conference on AI-Driven VLSI and Smart Communication Systems aims to bring together leading academicians, researchers, industry experts, and scholars to exchange knowledge and share innovative research in the fields of Artificial Intelligence, VLSI design, and smart communication technologies.

To promote global collaboration and wider participation, ICAIVSC 2026 will be conducted in hybrid mode (Online & Offline).

Our Mission

To provide a premier interdisciplinary platform for researchers, practitioners, educators, and industry professionals to present and discuss the latest innovations, trends, research challenges, and technological advancements in AI-integrated VLSI and intelligent communication systems.

Our Scope

The conference focuses on AI-driven VLSI design, intelligent semiconductor systems, low-power chip architectures, AI-enabled 5G/6G communication networks, Edge AI, IoT, embedded systems, and advanced signal processing technologies.

Organizing Institution

R.M.K. Engineering College

Established in 1995, R.M.K. Engineering College is an autonomous institution affiliated to Anna University, Chennai, approved by AICTE, and accredited by NAAC with A+ Grade. Located in Kavaraipettai, Tamil Nadu, the college offers a strong academic environment with modern infrastructure and advanced research facilities

Publication & Indexing

All accepted and presented papers are expected to be published in AIP conference proceedings. The papers will also be submitted for indexing in Scopus.
