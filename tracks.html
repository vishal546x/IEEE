Technical Tracks

ICAIVSC 2026 focuses on both theory and applications. We invite original research papers on the following technical tracks:

01

AI in VLSI Design

Application of artificial intelligence techniques for efficient chip design, testing, and optimization.

02

Advanced VLSI Systems

Design and development of high-performance and next-generation integrated circuits.

03

Low-Power Chip Design

Energy-efficient architectures and techniques for sustainable semiconductor systems.

04

AI in Communication Systems

Integration of AI to enhance wireless, mobile, and next-generation communication networks.

05

Wireless & Optical Communication

Innovations in modern wireless, satellite, and fiber-optic communication technologies.

06

Edge AI and IoT Systems

Intelligent embedded platforms enabling real-time processing at the edge.

07

AI-Based Signal Processing

Use of machine learning and deep learning for advanced signal analysis and applications.

08

Embedded and Smart Systems

Development of intelligent hardware-software systems for smart applications.

Publication: All accepted and presented papers are expected to be published in AIP conference proceedings. The papers will also be submitted for indexing in Scopus.
