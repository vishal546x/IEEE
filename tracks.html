Technical Tracks

ICSVCE 2021 focuses on both theory and applications. We invite original research papers on the following technical tracks:

01

Signal Processing

Advanced theories and applications in digital and analog signal analysis.

02

VLSI Design

Innovations in Very Large Scale Integration and circuit architecture.

03

Communication Systems

Modern wireless, satellite, and optical communication frameworks.

04

RF and Microwave Systems

High-frequency systems, antenna design, and electromagnetic theory.

05

Embedded Systems

Real-time operating systems, IoT, and hardware-software co-design.

Publication: All accepted and presented papers are expected to be published in Springer Conference Proceedings (Approval Pending) and indexed in Scopus.
